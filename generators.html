The Motomi Process: Diesel Engines

Install your MOTOMI re-manufactured diesel engines with confidence. Every MOTOMI diesel engine is backed by a Nation wide warranty of up to 6 months. Here's the process we go through to ensure that kind of reliability. Our optional Premium Service Plan provides allowances for such items as towing, car rental, fluids and premium labor should a problem arise.

1 thorough cleaning

Every diesel engine is completely disassembled. Blocks, heads, rods, crankshafts and camshafts are thoroughly cleaned to remove all contaminants which can lead to early component and engine failure.

2 crankshafts

Crankshafts are precision machined to exact tolerances and polished to a micro finish, then inspected with a profilometer. Diesel engine crankshafts are also inspected for magnetism and demagnetized so as to not attract metal particles generated by normal engine break-in.

3 connecting rods

Connecting rods in JASPER's diesel engines are cleaned, then machined to O.E.M. specifications and honed to the standard diameter of original equipment for even distribution of stress on the bearings. New bushings are installed and machined in all bushed rods.

4 Heads

Heads are resurfaced to assure a proper sealing surface with the block and a finish compatible with today's new gasket materials. Valve height is miked and every head is vacuum tested to assure correct valve seating.

5 Heads

Blocks are magnetic particle inspected for cracks or imperfections. Cylinders are bored and honed (as applicable to engine design) and gauged to precise tolerances. Computer controlled equipment is used during boring to maintain correct cylinder-to-crankshaft alignment for smooth piston travel.

6 Heads

Fuel components are remanufactured by skilled technicians in a climate controlled and air filtered environment. Fuel injection pumps and injectors (both mechanical and electronic) are remanufactured, tested and calibrated on state of the art equipment or replaced with new.

7 Heads

Camshafts are inspected for magnetism, demagnetized and precisely machined to provide correct lift.
