AT MOtomi

MOTOMI is a repair and maintenance company, incorporated in 2013. The company fixes a wide range of vehicles, generators and heavy equipment from German engineered BMW to American Cummins. Equipped with modern software, customers are assured of proper diagnosis and repairs.

It started out as a shop for vehicle repair and maintenance, managed by trained technicians with 15 years of experience in vehicle maintenance and repair. For years the company has focused solely on vehicles but more recently it has branched out into diesel engines for generators and heavy-duty machines. The newly emerged subsidiary of the business is aimed at aftermarket repair and maintenance for diesel generator.
